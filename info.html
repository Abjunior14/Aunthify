How to use the Aunthify

Follow these instructions to calculate the area of a rectangle:

  1. Access the Calculator:Click on the Calculator link in the navigation menu or click here
  2. Enter the Length: Input the length of the rectangle in the "Length" feild.
  3. Enter the Width: Input the width of the rectangle in the "Width" feild.
  4. Calculate the Area: Click the "Calculate" button to compute the area.
  5. View the Result: The area will be displayed below the "width" feild.
